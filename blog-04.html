    Thalapathy Vijay's 'Beast to release on April 13

    While there has been speculation that Thalapathy Vijay's 'Beast' will be released on the weekend of Tamil NÃ©w Year, it has now been officially announced by Sun Pictures that the film will actually release on April 13. Directed by Nelson Dilipkumar, the film features Pooja Hegde and Selvaraghavan in the main roles. Considered a comedy, the film was certified with a U / A certificate. In fact, fans of the actor cracked the CBFC website to get censor details recently. The duration of the film has been revealed and 'The Beast' will be 2 hours and 35 minutes long, 15 minutes shorter than his previous films. And, as promised, the fans got to know that the trailer of Beast will be unveiled on April 2.

    'Arabic Kuthu' from 'Beast' sets the internet on fire!

    The recording of Vijay's film 'Beast' has been completed, and the film is in the process of being released. His producers started promoting the film by releasing the first song 'Arabic Kuthu' a few days ago, and the song has become a song, viral hit. Now, Arabic Kuthu becomes the fastest-running Tamil song with 100 million YouTube views. Since the release of Arabic Kuthu, a Tamil peppy song composed by Anirudh Ravichander has been popular online, and the song has lively lyrics all over the world. The massive collaboration has made the song set a new record as it surpasses 100 million views in 12 days 7 hours on YouTube, and becomes the fastest Tamil song to achieve this music.
